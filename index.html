Carlo Ancelotti(Coach)

Carlo Ancelotti ( born 10 June 1959 Reggiolo , Emilia-Romagna ) is an Italian football coach who previously played as a central midfielder. Since 2021, he has been the head coach of the Spanish club Real Madrid .

Dani Carvajal

Dani Carvajal ( Spanish: Dani Carvajal , full name Daniel Carvajal Ramos ; pronunciation : born 11 January 1992 , Leganés , Madrid ) is a Spanish footballer who plays as a full back for Real Madrid and the Spain national team .

Jude Bellingham

Jude Victor William Bellingham ( born 29 June 2003 ) is an English footballer who plays as a midfielder for Spanish club Real Madrid and the England national team . In April 2024, he won the Breakthrough of the Year award from the Laureus World Sports Awards.

Kylian Mbappe

Kylian Mbappé Lottin (born 20 December 1998, Paris ) is a French footballer who plays as a forward for Real Madrid and captains the France national team . He is considered one of the best players in the world . He was the world champion and the best young player of the 2018 World Cup . At the 2022 World Cup, he became the top scorer (8 goals).

Vinisius Junior

Vinícius José Paixão de Oliveira Júnior ( ; born 12 July 2000, São Gonçalo , Rio de Janeiro ), commonly known as Vinícius Júnior , is a Brazilian and Spanish footballer who plays as a forward for Real Madrid and the Brazil national team .
